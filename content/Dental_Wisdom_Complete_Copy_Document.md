## 2. FAQ Page (`faq.html`)

### When is the Dental Wisdom Conference?

The Dental Wisdom Conference will take place March 3–6, 2027 at The Altair Hotel in Bay Harbor Islands, Miami.

### Where is the conference held?

The entire event will be held at The Altair Hotel, a beautiful waterfront boutique hotel located at: 9540 West Bay Harbor Drive, Bay Harbor Islands, FL 33154. All lectures, hands-on classes, meals, and social events take place on-site at the hotel.

### How do I get to the hotel?

Most attendees fly into Miami International Airport (MIA) or Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL).

- MIA is approximately 15 miles (about 24 minutes drive) from the hotel.
- FLL is about 20–25 miles away. Uber, taxi, or rideshare are the easiest options (no official shuttle is provided).

### What should I expect as a first-time attendee?

The Dental Wisdom Conference is intentionally intimate, warm, and unhurried. Expect high-quality cross-specialty lectures and hands-on education in a relaxed atmosphere with built-in time for reflection and genuine connection with colleagues. The event includes beautiful minyanim, inspiring shiurim, glatt kosher meals, and uplifting Shabbos programming. Many attendees bring their spouses and children. The overall vibe is professional yet welcoming and community-oriented — designed for both professional growth and personal rejuvenation in a warm Jewish environment.

### What happens after I register?

After you complete your registration, you will receive an immediate receipt and confirmation email. If you selected hotel accommodations during registration, you will receive a separate email from The Altair Hotel to finalize your room booking. As the conference approaches, you will receive additional communications with important details, including how to optionally reserve Shabbos meals for you and your family.

### What is the daily schedule?

Please note that the schedule below is tentative. Organizers reserve the right to make adjustments as needed. The final schedule will be published closer to the event.

- **Wednesday:** Welcome event for adults at 8:30 PM
- **Thursday:** Daf Yomi at 6 AM, Davening at 7 AM, Breakfast at 8 AM, Lectures 9 AM–12 PM, Lunch, Afternoon lectures 1 PM–7:30 PM, followed by 8 PM party with glatt kosher dinner and music.
- **Friday:** Daf Yomi at 6 AM, Davening at 7 AM, Breakfast at 8 AM, Lectures 9 AM–12 PM, Lunch, Afternoon lectures until Shabbos. Davening, Shabbos dinner, Oneg, and shiurim in the evening.
- **Shabbos:** Breakfast at 8:30 AM, Davening at 9 AM, Lunch at 12 PM, Afternoon shiurim, Mincha, Shalosh Seudos, Maariv, followed by a closing party.

### Who is lecturing and what classes will be offered?

We are actively curating and selecting great speakers from all dental specialties, staying consistent with our approach from previous conferences. The program will feature a mix of standard lectures, smaller intimate classes, and hands-on sessions. If you are interested in lecturing, please apply here. A full list of lectures and class topics will be published closer to the event.

### Who is this event for?

This conference is designed for Jewish dentists who want to combine high-quality continuing education with meaningful Jewish community. Expect inspiring shiurim, beautiful minyanim, cross-specialty lectures, hands-on CE, delicious glatt kosher meals, uplifting Shabbos programming, and genuine connections within the Dental Wisdom community.

### What is the format of the event?

This is a small, intimate, invitation-style continuing education event with limited attendance. You can expect interactive lectures, hands-on classes, and plenty of time for networking over meals and social events.

### How many CE credits will be offered?

20 CE credits will be offered in accordance with AGD PACE guidelines. Final credit hours are subject to change.

### Is this event AGD PACE approved?

Yes, the course is hosted by an AGD PACE approved provider.

### How does registration work?

Due to limited capacity, early interest does not guarantee a seat. Your registration is only confirmed once payment is received and you receive an email confirmation from the organizers.

### Is the hotel included in the registration fee?

No, hotel accommodations are not included in the registration fee. We have secured a special room block at The Altair Hotel. During the registration process, you will be able to select your preferred dates and room type directly. Availability is limited and subject to the hotel’s conference block.

**Available Room Rates (per night):**
- Studio King: ~$400
- Studio Queen: ~$400
- 1 Bedroom Suite: ~$600
- 2 Bedroom Suite: ~$950

A one-night deposit is required at the time of booking.

### Can I get a bigger room or extend my stay?

Yes. You can upgrade to a larger room or extend your stay beyond the conference dates. During registration you can select your preferred dates and room type. If you’d like to upgrade or add extra nights after registering, just let us know and we’ll coordinate directly with the hotel (subject to availability and hotel policies).

### How much does the Dental Wisdom Conference cost for Dentists to attend?

- Dentist: $1,995
- Dental Student or Resident: $1,595
- Vendor Dentist: $2,995

### How much does it cost for my spouse and children to attend?

- Spouse: $450
- Child (age 3 and older): $100

### What does registration include?

**For Dentists:**
- All lectures and CE credits
- Coffee and snacks throughout the program
- Welcome event (Wednesday evening)
- Glatt kosher lunches (Thursday & Friday)
- Thursday night dinner + music
- Friday night Oneg
- Shalosh Seudos
- Motzei Shabbos closing event with dinner + music

**For Registered Family Members (Spouse & Children):**
- Children’s activities (Thursday & Friday)
- Glatt kosher lunches (Thursday & Friday)
- Thursday night dinner + music
- Friday night Oneg
- Shalosh Seudos
- Motzei Shabbos closing event with dinner + music

### Early Bird / Discount Deadline

We reserve the right to raise pricing dynamically based on demand due to limited space. Register early to lock in the current rates.

### What is the cancellation and refund policy?

All registration fees are non-refundable except in cases of documented medical emergencies (doctor’s note required, subject to Organizers’ reasonable approval). Registrations are non-transferable and no substitutions or name changes are permitted.

### Can I attend without staying at the host hotel?

We strongly recommend staying at The Altair Hotel for the full conference experience. As a bonus, the first 25 attendees who book a room at the hotel will receive a complimentary bottle of wine for Shabbos.

### Will there be sponsors or exhibitors?

A limited number of aligned sponsors may support the event. Sponsorship does not influence the educational content.

### Will sessions be recorded?

Sessions will not be recorded. In-person attendance is required to receive CE credits.

### What is the dress code?

Business casual to professional attire is recommended. Many attendees wear suits, blazers, and smart dresses. We encourage everyone to look polished and professional.

### How will CE attendance be verified?

Attendance verification and course evaluations are required to receive CE credit. Details will be provided during the event.

### Who can I contact with questions?

Please email info@dentalwisdom.org.

### Accessibility

We are committed to making the Dental Wisdom Conference and website accessible to all participants. The venue complies with applicable ADA requirements. We provide reasonable accommodations upon advance request. Please contact us at info@dentalwisdom.org at least 30 days prior to the event to discuss your needs.

**Headline:** Sponsorship Opportunities

Sponsorship opportunities for the Dental Wisdom Conference are intentionally limited and thoughtfully integrated to preserve the integrity of the experience.

### Sponsor Questions & Answers

**What types of sponsors are you looking for?**  
We are seeking a small, carefully selected group of sponsors who genuinely support high-quality, evidence-based dental education and respect a distraction-free learning environment. We especially welcome companies that are already trusted and recommended by members of the Dental Wisdom community.

**How many attendees will be at the event?**  
Attendance is intentionally limited to approximately 100 participants to maintain an intimate, high-touch, and interactive experience.

**What are the different sponsorship tiers?**  
We offer several sponsorship levels with varying benefits. Full details, including benefits and pricing, are available in our sponsorship prospectus. Please contact us at info@dentalwisdom.org to receive it.

**What sponsor exposure is included?**  
Benefits vary by tier and may include logo placement on event materials and website, verbal acknowledgments, co-branded communications, and presence in the designated sponsor area.

**When do I need to confirm my sponsorship by?**  
The sooner you confirm your sponsorship, the sooner your company will be included on the website, event materials, and printed collateral. We recommend securing your sponsorship as early as possible to maximize visibility.

**Can I get exclusivity?**  
Yes, category exclusivity is available on select tiers. Please reach out to us with your specific exclusivity requests, and we will do our best to accommodate them.

**Which events at the conference can be sponsored?**  
Many events and sessions throughout the conference can be sponsored, including:
- Lectures (via honorariums)
- Breakfast, lunch, and snacks
- Evening parties
- Shabbos Oneg
- Shalosh Seudos

Additional sponsorship opportunities may be available depending on the tier.

**Will sponsors be allowed to present or sell during the event?**  
Sponsorship packages may include limited promotional speaking time depending on the tier. All educational content remains fully independent. Depending on sponsorship tier, designated vendor tables will be available for product displays and informal conversations.

**Does sponsorship include attendance?**  
Most packages include complimentary registration for sponsor representatives, giving you access to networking events, meals, and social functions.

**How is educational integrity protected?**  
All lectures and hands-on classes are developed independently by the organizers and fully comply with AGD PACE standards. Sponsors have no influence over content or clinical recommendations.

**What are the rules for vendor tables and booth setup?**  
Vendor table setup is available depending on your sponsorship tier. Sponsors granted table space will receive one folding table. You are responsible for providing your own extension cords if needed. Power outlets will be reasonably accessible.

**Can sponsors send materials in advance?**  
Yes. Detailed guidance on shipping deadlines and procedures will be provided closer to the event.

**Can sponsors provide materials or branded items?**  
Yes. Depending on the tier, sponsors may include approved product samples, educational materials, or branded items in welcome bags or for distribution.

**Will sponsors receive attendee contact information?**  
Attendee contact information is shared only on an opt-in basis and in full compliance with privacy regulations.

**What is the value of sponsoring this event?**  
This intimate gathering offers meaningful connections with engaged Jewish dentists — many of whom are practice owners and key decision-makers. You gain authentic alignment with a trusted, high-quality community rather than broad mass exposure.

**What are the sponsorship fees?**  
Fees vary by tier and level of involvement. Please email info@dentalwisdom.org for the current sponsorship prospectus and pricing details.

**What is the sponsorship cancellation policy?**  
Terms will be clearly outlined in the sponsorship agreement. Fees are generally non-refundable once the agreement is signed and confirmed.

**Who should we contact regarding sponsorship?**  
Please email info@dentalwisdom.org.

## 4. Want to Lecture Page (`conference-new-speaker.html`)

**Headline:** Lecturing Opportunities

Lecturing opportunities at the Dental Wisdom Conference are intentionally limited and hand-picked from within our own community. We carefully select speakers who embody the values, clinical excellence, and spirit of the Dental Wisdom network.

**Button:** EMAIL US TODAY → info@dentalwisdom.org

### Lecture Questions & Answers

**Who is eligible to lecture at the Dental Wisdom Conference?**  
We primarily select lecturers from within our vibrant Dental Wisdom community — a trusted network of approximately 900+ Jewish dentists. We strongly prefer speakers who are active members of the community or have been directly referred by an active member.

**How are speakers selected?**  
The organizers personally review all interested lecturers and carefully curate a balanced program that includes large lectures, intimate smaller classes, and hands-on courses. Final decisions on speakers, topics, and formats are made by the organizers to ensure every session delivers practical, high-value skills that attendees can implement immediately in their practice.

**What types of lectures and classes are you looking for?**  
We are looking for cutting-edge, practical, and multi-specialty topics across all areas of dentistry. We welcome both clinical and practice-management focused presentations. We also welcome dental-related shiurim that combine Torah perspectives with professional growth and ethical practice.

Lecturers are warmly encouraged to connect us with potential vendors or companies they work with that may be interested in sponsoring the event. In many cases, we can offer an honorarium for lectures, particularly when linked to a sponsorship.

**How many speakers do you typically select?**  
We typically select between 10–15 speakers in total to fill three full days of programming.

**What should be included in a lecture proposal?**  
No need to prepare anything formal yet. Simply reach out with a working topic, a short description and any relevant speaking experiences. It is also very helpful if you let us know if there are any companies you currently work with that might be interested in sponsoring the event and providing an honorarium.

**What is the process to apply to lecture?**  
Simply reach out to us directly at info@dentalwisdom.org. If you are an active member of the Dental Wisdom WhatsApp group, you may also message the event organizer directly there.

**Is there a deadline to submit a lecture proposal?**  
There is no strict deadline, but we encourage you to reach out as early as possible. Early submissions receive priority as we build the program.

**What are the benefits of being a speaker?**  
Speaking at the Dental Wisdom Conference is a meaningful way to give back to the community, share your expertise, help younger dentists grow, and strengthen connections within our network.

**Will speakers receive compensation or honorarium?**  
Honorariums are offered on a case-by-case basis depending on the topic, length, and format of the presentation. Some speakers may also receive partial or full complimentary registration. Many lecturers also choose to sponsor the event through their companies or brands.

**How long are the lecture slots?**  
Lecture slots typically range from 1 to 4 hours, depending on the topic and format.

**Do you accept hands-on or workshop-style presentations?**  
Yes. We welcome hands-on workshops and interactive sessions, as they are a valuable part of the conference experience.

**What AV / technical support is provided?**  
A dedicated AV team will be on-site to support all speakers throughout the conference.

**Can I promote my products or services during my lecture?**  
All lectures must comply with AGD PACE guidelines. Promotional lectures focused primarily on selling products or services are not aligned with the educational integrity of this event.

**Will my lecture be recorded?**  
Lectures are generally not recorded. In-person attendance is required to receive CE credits.

**When will speakers be notified if their proposal is accepted?**  
We aim to finalize the program as early as possible. Early applicants will be notified first.

**Can I co-present with another dentist?**  
Yes. If you would like to co-present with another dentist, please mention this when you reach out so we can discuss the opportunity together.

**Do you provide speaker preparation support or coaching?**  
Yes. We are happy to discuss potential topics with you and provide feedback to ensure your presentation aligns with the goals and standards of the conference.

**Where can I submit my lecture proposal?**  
Please email your proposal or inquiry to info@dentalwisdom.org.
