About the Conference

The Dental Wisdom Conference was created to give Jewish dentists a chance to reset — to engage with meaningful, high-quality continuing education while finding calm and clarity.

Set at The Altair Hotel in Bay Harbor Islands, the location is as intentional as the experience itself. An event designed for professional excellence, spiritual growth, and lasting community connection.

Our third year hosting this extraordinary event.
