Created by Jewish Dentists,

Conference Details

Location & Dates

The 2027 Dental Wisdom Conference will be held at The Altair Hotel, from Wednesday evening, March 3, through Saturday evening, March 6, 2027. We have made arrangements with the hotel for preferred pricing for conference attendees. During registration you will select which rooms you would like booked.

Class Options & CE Credits

The 2027 Dental Wisdom Conference will offer an estimated 20 CE credits, accredited through the Academy of General Dentistry (AGD). Lectures will be cross-specialty, include lecture and hands-on options, with multiple simultaneous class options when scheduling permits.

Lecture topics, speakers and schedule will be published closer to the event.

Hotel Accommodations
