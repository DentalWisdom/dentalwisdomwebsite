Conference Details

Location & Dates

The 2027 Dental Wisdom Conference will be held at The Altair Hotel, from Wednesday evening, March 3, through Saturday evening, March 6, 2027. We have made arrangements with the hotel for preferred pricing for conference attendees. During registration you will select which rooms you would like booked.

Class Options & CE Credits

The 2027 Dental Wisdom Conference will offer an estimated 20 CE credits, that will be accredited through the Academy of General Dentistry (AGD). Lectures will be cross-specialty, include lecture and hands-on options, with multiple simultaneous class options when scheduling permits.

Lecture topics, speakers and schedule will be published closer to the event.

Hotel Accommodations

Dental Wisdom has secured a preferred room block with special rates at The Altair Hotel in Bay Harbor Islands, Miami, exclusively for conference attendees.

During the registration process, you will be able to select and book your preferred room type directly. Availability is limited and subject to the hotel's conference block.

Studio King ~$400/night
Studio Queen ~$400/night
1 Bed Suite ~$600/night
2 Bed Suite ~$950/night

One night deposit is required at time of booking.

Davening & Shabbos

Daily Minyanim are offered on-site for Shacharis, Mincha and Maariv. Optional Shabbos meals are being arranged for all conference attendees.

During the registration process you will indicate if you would like to join Shabbos meals.
