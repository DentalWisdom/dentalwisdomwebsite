Become a Deals Partner

List your company on Dental Wisdom Deals and get your products and services in front of a targeted, engaged community of dental professionals.

Dental Wisdom Deals came out of a conversation at our first annual conference in 2025. A room of dentists — people who knew each other, referred to each other, and were buying from many of the same vendors — recognized that as a community, they had real leverage. Not to squeeze anyone, but to build something more valuable: a shared list of trusted companies, preferred pricing negotiated on the community’s behalf, and an easy way to find reliable partners without starting from scratch every time.

That’s what this is. A curated collection of offers from companies the Dental Wisdom network recommends — available to every member, at no cost to dentists. Know a company worth adding? Tell us and we’ll reach out.

Why Dental Wisdom Deals Exists

When someone in the Dental Wisdom community recommends a vendor, it means something. It’s a colleague vouching for a company they actually use — not a sponsored post or a generic referral list.

Every partner in Dental Wisdom Deals has been recommended by a member of the community. Every deal has been negotiated to offer real value. These aren’t random promotions — they’re introductions to companies that have earned the trust of people like you.

How It Works

  • We identify companies that members trust and would genuinely recommend to a colleague.
  • We reach out directly and negotiate preferred pricing or exclusive terms on the community’s behalf.
  • Members get access to the full list — preferred pricing, trusted introductions, all in one place.
  • There is no cost to dentists to participate.

For Dentists

Dental Wisdom Deals is open to everyone — you don’t need to be a member to access the deals. If you found this page, the deals are yours to use.

Looking for reliable partners? Browse our growing list of curated deals from vendors and service providers recommended by fellow Dental Wisdom members.

Know a company we should consider? If you have a vendor, supplier, or service provider you’d recommend to a colleague, let us know. We’ll reach out to explore potential exclusive offers for the community.

For Companies & Sponsors

We’re interested in building long-term, mutually beneficial relationships with companies that genuinely want to support dental professionals.

If your company would like to be considered for Dental Wisdom Deals, we invite you to reach out. We carefully evaluate potential partners based on quality, reliability, and alignment with our community.

Interested in partnering with us? Please email us at info@dentalwisdom.org with the subject line “Deals Partnership Inquiry” and tell us a bit about your company and what you offer.
